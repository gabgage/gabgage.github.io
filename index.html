About Me
Welcome to my Digital Portfolio! Here you can get a better understanding as to who I am both as an academic as well as a person.
To begin, my name is Gabriella Gage and I am currently a Junior attending The University of Colorado Boulder pursuing a Computer Science degree. Throughout my schooling I have been able to explore many different paths within Computer Science, but it wasn't until UI/UX development that I found my calling. I have always loved to be creative with different aspects of my life, and have found that website development can be my own personal playground for testing out new techniques. Through one of the software development classes I have taken, I have been exposed to different resources like that of Figma and different deployment tools that have helped me be able to create and launch my own projects. Throughout my education I have been able to participate in teams and work solo, ensuring I can not only be a team player but also a confident coder on my own.
Outside of my educational career I have a love for snowboarding, or anything that involves being in the mountains. As a Colorado native, I was practically raised on skis, therefore any chance I get you can find me in the snow. While I am currently focused on my studies, I want to be able to explore the world and learn as much as I can about different cultures.
Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about me! I am always interested in learning as much as I can about any aspect of Computer Science, so please feel free to reach out to me!
As ever,
