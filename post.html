About Me!

Throughout my schooling I have been able to explore many different paths within Computer Science, but it wasn't until UI/UX development that I found my calling. I have always loved to be creative with different aspects of my life, and have found that front-end website development can be my own personal playground for testing out new techniques. Through one of the software development classes I have taken, I have been exposed to different resources like that of Figma and different deployment tools that have helped me be able to create and launch my own projects. Throughout my education I have been able to participate in teams and work solo, ensuring I can not only be a team player but also a confident coder on my own.

Outside of my educational career, I am deeply family oriented and have a love of snowboarding, or practically anything that involves being in the mountains. As a Colorado native, I was raised on skis, therefore any chance I get you can find me in the snow. While I am currently focused on my studies, I want to be able to visit more countries and learn as much as I can about different cultures, making one of my goals to be able to explore the world.

During the COVID-19 lockdown, I also discovered my green thumb, and amassed a collection of houseplants that I still care for to this day. Gardening quickly became one of my favorite hobbies, as there is always a ton of excitement learning about new plants and how their needs differ from those I already have. I have found that there is a certain level of peace that can be found in caring for plants that I have not been able to replicate in any other form of hobby!

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about me! I am always interested in learning as much as I can about any aspect of Computer Science, so please feel free to reach out to me!

Regards, Gabriella
